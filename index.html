A New Kind of RPG

Speak, and the World Listens

In a world shaped by ancient magic and fading memory, you awaken as a stranger — pulled from another reality into the land of Eruin. The townsfolk have never seen anyone like you. They'll have questions. And so will you.

Eruin is a story-driven RPG where every NPC is powered by AI. Speak to them with your own voice. Ask anything. Confide, interrogate, befriend, or deceive. Their responses are unscripted, shaped by their personalities, their knowledge of the world — and their memory of you.

Core Pillars

What Makes Eruin Different

Voice-Driven Conversations

Speak naturally to any NPC using your microphone. AI-powered speech recognition and text-to-speech create fluid, real-time dialogue that feels alive.

Living, Remembering NPCs

Every character has their own knowledge, personality, and lore. They remember what you've told them. Your choices and words ripple through the world.

A Handcrafted Fantasy

A lovingly built world with quests, lore, and mysteries — not procedurally generated content. AI enriches the story, it doesn't replace the storytelling.
